• Ingredients Of

  • Rukak

  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (optional)
  4. - Water (as needed)

  • Mix the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. If you're using oil, add it in as well.
  2. Gradually add water, mixing until a soft dough forms.

  • Knead the Dough

  1. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 30 minutes.

  • Roll Out the Dough

  1. Divide the dough into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
  2. On a floured surface, roll each ball out as thin as possible (similar to
    3. making tortillas).

  • Cook the Ruqaq

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or flat pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook each piece of rolled dough for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or
    3. until lightly browned and cooked through.

  • Keep Warm

  1. Place the cooked Ruqaq on a plate and cover with a clean towel to keep
    2. warm.

  • Serve

  1. Serve with your favorite filling or alongside stews and soups.
