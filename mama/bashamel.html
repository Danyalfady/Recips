Makarona Bashamel

  • Ingrediants of Makarona Bashmel

  • For Pasta

  1. 500g macaroni (penne or any tubular pasta)

  2. Salt (for boiling the pasta)

  3. tablespoon of oil (for boiling)

  • For the Meat Filling:

  1. 500g minced beef or lamb

  2. onion, finely chopped

  3. tablespoons vegetable oil

  4. teaspoon ground black pepper

  5. teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

  6. Salt to taste

  7. tablespoon tomato paste (optional)

  • For the Béchamel Sauce:

  1. cups of milk
  2. tablespoons all-purpose flour
  3. tablespoons butter
  4. Salt and pepper to taste
  5. A pinch of nutmeg (optional)

  • For the Topping:

  1. egg (optional, to be mixed into the béchamel sauce for a richer texture)
  2. 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella or Parmesan cheese (optional)
  3. And put in oven for about 20 minute
