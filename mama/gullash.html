• Ingrediants OF

  • GULLASH

  1. 500 grams of phyllo pastry (goulash sheets)
  2. 200 grams of ghee or butter
  3. - 500 grams of minced meat (beef or chicken, as preferred)
  4. - 1 large onion, chopped
  5. Salt and pepper to taste
  6. Spices (like cinnamon or cumin)
  7. 2 eggs (optional)
  8. - 1 cup of milk

  • Prepare the Filling

  1. In a pan over medium heat, melt the ghee, then add the chopped onion
    2. and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add the minced meat and cook until browned.
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and spices, then let it cool.

  • Prepare the Phyllo

  1. Grease a baking dish with some ghee.
  2. Start by laying one sheet of phyllo pastry and brush it with ghee. Repeat
    3. this process with 5-6 sheets
  3. Spread a layer of the meat filling on top of the phyllo.

  • Repeat the Process

  1. Place 2-3 more sheets of phyllo brushed with ghee on top of the filling,
    2. then add another layer of filling.
  2. Continue this layering until you finish the filling and phyllo, ending with
    3. a top layer of phyllo.

  • Bake

  1. Before placing it in the oven, mix the eggs with the milk and brush this
    2. mixture over the top layer of phyllo.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F), then bake for 30-40 minutes or until
    3. the top is golden brown.

  • Serve

  1. Once slightly cooled, cut the goulash into pieces and serve warm.

Enjoy your meal!
