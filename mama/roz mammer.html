    Roz Mammer

  1. 2 cups Egyptian rice
  2. 3 cups milk
  3. 1 cup chicken or beef broth (or water as a substitute)
  4. 2 tablespoons butter or ghee
  5. Salt to taste
  6. A pinch of black pepper (optional)
  7. 1/2 cup cream (for a golden top layer)

  • Prepare the Rice:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and let it drain.

  • Mix the Ingredients

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the milk, broth, butter,
    2. 2. salt, and black pepper. Stir well until the butter melts into the mixture.

  • Layer The Rice

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). In an ovenproof
    2. dish, spread the washed rice evenly.

  • Add the Liquid

  1. Pour the milk and broth mixture over the rice in the
    2. baking dish. Make sure the rice is fully submerged in the liquid.

  • Add Cream for a Golden Crust

  1. Spread the cream evenly over the top of
    2. the mixture for a rich, golden crust.

  • Bake

  1. Place the dish in the preheated oven and bake for about 60-75
    2. minutes, or until the rice absorbs the liquid and the top becomes golden brown.

  • Serve:

  1. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving, then enjoy the
    2. creamy, rich Roz Mammar.
